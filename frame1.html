Zoomdash

A zooming dashboard in your browser to present your applications' built in dashboards at once

Welcome to ZoomDash

Do you have a TV presenting an application dashboard like Zabbix, New Relic, What's Up Gold, etc? Have you ever wished you could see more than one dashboard at a time? That's what ZoomDash was built for. It toggles between a small view of 9 dahsboards and a full view of each, zooming around with a hopefully smooth animation
